At any point I am wearing an exclamation point headband, people can ask me for a quest.  If they do, I give them a QR code card that brings them to a warcraft styled quest list, with 3 tiers of quests.  Bringing me proof of completion of one or more quests for each tier earns that tier's quest reward.  Uncommon, rare, epic bags I will have with me ready to hand out.

The epic bag will contain a second QR code card to a repeatable quest, which is a static web page "world boss" that spawns/changes every 30 minutes and takes the form of one of several minigames chosen at random.  Completions lead to a google form backed database, with a frontend leaderboard displaying alongside the current world boss.  Rank is kept as "item level" and follows raidbots parse color schmes.  A user can go to their own profile to check their parses.  A parse is a record of a completed world boss, and their completed number of world bosses rank as percentile at time of completion.  Thus they will be able to see they were in the 99th percentile of completing world boss #3, and have a gold parse.  But with world boss #4 they completed it 25% percentile.  World bosses they didn't complete do not show up as parses.
