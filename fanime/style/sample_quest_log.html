Available quests

Bring snacks to the volunteer desk Feed the crew running panels.

Description

The crew running panels hasn’t eaten in six hours. Someone has to keep them upright until closing.

Objectives

  • Purchase or pack sealed snacks allowed by venue policy.
  • Deliver to the volunteer desk during staffed hours.
  • Show proof to the quest giver (photo at desk or receipt + timestamp).
Photo scavenger hunt (10 items) Find and photograph themed booth moments.

Description

A lighthearted excuse to see the floor. All shots must include your badge (blur personal info if needed).

Objectives

  • Collect 10 photos from the checklist in the ops doc.
  • Keep it PG and respect cosplayers’ consent.
  • Show the album or scroll-through to the quest giver.
Trade a skill for 15 minutes Teach origami, a card game, or line tricks.

Description

Help another attendee learn something small in a public space—hallway or designated chill zone, not loud panels.

Objectives

  • Pair up with a willing participant (no coercion).
  • Spend ~15 minutes teaching something you’re confident in.
  • Have them tell the quest giver one thing they learned (brief proof).

Proof & reward

Return to the quest giver with proof for this tier’s bag. They’ll hand you the Rare QR card for the next tier.
